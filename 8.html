Electric Circuits

An electric circuit is a path in which electrons flow.
When the current flows from positive terminal to negative terminal
of a power source, the circuit is said to be complete.
If this path is interrupted (like,open switch, incomplete wires, etc,.)
the circuit is said to be broken or incomplete.
Many number of devices can be connected to a circuit through parallel or series connection.
A circuit can be described through a circuit diagram.
A circuit diadram consists of symbols.
