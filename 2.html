Light

It is impossible to imagine life without light.
Light from sun is the main source of energy for humans.
We see things around us due to light rays that enter our eyes.
White light is made up of seven different colours:
Red,Orange,Yellow,Green,Blue,Indigo,Violet.
Light has got both particle as well as wave nature.
When light passes through prism it splits into its 7 constituent colours.
This is how rainbow is formed.
Unlike sound light does not need a medium to propogate.
But it propogate with different speeds in different media.
This causes refraction.
Twinkling of stars is caused due to atmospheric refraction.
