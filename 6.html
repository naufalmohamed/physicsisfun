Motion,Force and Friction

Force is a push or a pull that changes the
shape, size and state of an object.
Motion is caused due to force.
Friction is the force opposing motion from the opposite direction.
To make a stationary object move, force should be applied.
Inorder to increase the speed of a moving object,
force should be applied in the same direction of motion.
Inorder to stop a moving object force should be applied in the
direction opposite to motion.
