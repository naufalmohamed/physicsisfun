Heat

Heat is a form of energy.
It can be produced by friction, passing electricity through a resistor etc.,
The heat energy from sun is used in solar cookers.
Heat travels by three methods.
  1. conduction:
    This happens when two objects with different temperatures are in contact.
    Heat flows from the warmer to the cooler object.
  2. Convection:
    This is how heat travels in liquids and gases.
    Here cooler liquids or gases takes the place of warmer ones.
  3. Radiation:
    This is the transfer of heat from one object to another without any contact.
    Example, We feel sun's heat without even touching it.
